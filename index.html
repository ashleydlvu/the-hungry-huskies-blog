About Us

Hi! Our names are Ashley and Uzair and we are two hungry college students at the University of Washington on the hunt for the tasiest eats around! We started The Hungry Huskies to document some of our adventures as we try new cuisines in both local and abroad neighborhoods and hope that you enjoy our reviews and even try some of our recommendations! So sit back, grab a snack, and enjoy our foodie adventures...

Our adventures so far:

I was lucky enough to win a giveaway on IG to snag some of these highly anticipated @theflourbox donuts! I tried the London Fog, Cookies and Cream, and Hokey Pokey flavor and even managed to snag a cinnamon roll. The Cookies and Cream was by far my favorite (I'm probably biased tho because that is my go to flavor in everything). The brioche donut was perfectly pillowy but is very sweet (the sugar coating on the outside was a bit much for me). Would definetely sell my left kidney for the cinnamon roll. Overall would rate a 9.8/10
- Ashley
